About

                

                    Sapiens Optio is a free online Latin translator, powered by a dictionary of over 39,000 words,
                    originally compiled by William Whitaker.
                

                

                    The developer would like to thank William Whitaker, for developing the dictionary and licensing 3rd
                    party clients, of which this is one.

                    Latin and English dictionary used in this program is Copyright © 1993-2010 William
                    Whitaker

                    This application would not have been possible without the help and continual support of the
                    University of Notre Dame Archives, whose server originally hosted parts of this project and
                    Whitaker's dictionary.
